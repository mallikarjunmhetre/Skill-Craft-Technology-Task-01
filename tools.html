HOW VIGENÈRE WORKS

For keyword KEY:

Plaintext: H E L L O
Key repeats: K E Y K E
Shift values: 10 4 24 10 4
Ciphertext: R I J V S

Formula: C = (P + K) mod 26

Decrypt: P = (C - K + 26) mod 26

🛡️ Vigenère was considered unbreakable for 300 years. It was cracked by Charles Babbage in 1854.
